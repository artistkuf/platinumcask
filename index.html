INVESTING IN WHISKEY

Platinum Cask is offering a unique rare whisky opportunity. We are pioneering a concept we call “Cask Fractions”.

This is an opportunity for everyone to start their own cask whisky collection, with whisky from a huge variety of distilleries to choose from and for a very low price of purchase.

SINGLE CASK BOTTLES

Our single cask releases come from the most unique casks in our collection, bottled at cask strength and limited to the number of bottles that the chosen cask is able to fill. The older the cask the fewer bottles it will yield, depending on the type of cask we expect releases to be typically 100 to 300 bottles in total.
